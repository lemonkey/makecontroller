/**
  \page networking Networking
  
  \section Overview 
  The Make Controller comes with a fully loaded network system, including:
  - TCP sockets
  - TCP server sockets
  - UDP sockets
  - DHCP
  - DNS name resolution
  
  On top of these, additional services are provided:
  - simple web server
  - web client
  
  \section Breakdown
  
  If you want your board to head out onto the internet and connect to other sites or services, 
  check the WebClient class.
  
  To provide access to your board from the internet or from a web browser, 
  check the WebServer class.
  
  To make socket level connections, check the TcpSocket, TcpServer and UdpSocket classes.
  
  To configure your board's network settings (address, gateway, dhcp, dns), check the Network class.
*/
